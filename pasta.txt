Pasta w/Sauce

1. Boil Water and Put Sauce into large saute pan over low heat
2. When water starts to boil add salt and Pasta reduc heat to medium
3. Let Pasta cook for 5-12 minutes depending on type
4. When ready add some water from the pot to the sauce, and drain the rest
5. Add the drained pasta to the Saute pan, and cook over low for a few minutes folding the sauce into the pasta
